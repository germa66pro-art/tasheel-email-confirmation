Account Deletion Policy

Tasheel users can delete their account directly from inside the application.

To delete your account:

  1. Open the Tasheel app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Security
  4. Scroll below the "Change Password" section
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm your request

Once the deletion request is submitted, the account is immediately deactivated. All associated personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers after a 3-day grace period.

During the 3-day grace period, users may log back into their account to cancel the deletion request.
